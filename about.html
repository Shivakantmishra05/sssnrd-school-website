About Our Institution

Building future with education, values, and opportunity.

Who We Are

S.S.S.N.R.D. Inter College & Public School, located in Todarpur, District Mainpuri (U.P.), is committed to providing quality education to every child. Under the vision of “Har Ghar Shiksha Abhiyan”, our institution focuses on removing financial barriers so that no talented student is deprived of education.

From April 2026 onwards, we have introduced a revolutionary initiative where 100% tuition fees are waived. Education at our institution is driven by values, discipline, and holistic development.

Our Vision & Mission

🎯 To provide affordable, quality education to rural and semi-urban students.

🎯 To prepare students for academic excellence and competitive exams like Sainik School & Navodaya Vidyalaya.

🎯 To build responsible citizens with strong moral values.
